  • Add Events: Scroll to the bottom to add events to your personal calendar.
  • Delete/Edit Events: Click on a personal invite on your calendar to delete and edit your event.
  • Share Calendar: You may enter another registered user in order to share all your calendar invites with them. Users receiving your calendar will have your events displayed but will be unable to edit or delete your shared events.
  • Group Events/Invite Others:You may enter another registered user in order to invite them to your event. Any user is able to share a group event, regardless of who created the event. Group events may be deleted from your personal calendar, but will not be deleted from others. Group events may be edited by any user and this will be reflected on all calendars of invited users. You may not invite a user who has already been invited.
